Delicious Pancakes

Ingredients

  1. 1 cup of all purpose flour
  2. 2 tablespoons of sugar
  3. 1 tablespoon of baking powder
  4. 1/2 tablesppon of salt
  5. 1 cup of milk
  6. 1 egg
  7. 2 tablespoons melted butter

Instructions

  1. In a bowl,mix flour,sugar and baking powder
  2. I another bowl,mix egg with milk and melted butter
  3. Combine wet and dry ingredients and mix them
  4. Keep it in the oven
